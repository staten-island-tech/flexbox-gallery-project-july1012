These are the most refreshing fruits that could ever be:

(not in any specific order)

Watermelon

Watermelons are so refeshing especially after a long summer day. It literally has the word water in it so you know it will be juicy and perfect.

Mango

Mangos are just a classic that can never go wrong. esp ones from the motherland are on top

Grapes

Grapes are the best dessert you could ever have. but ONLY GREEN grapes are acceptable. Purple grapes are disgusting.

Peach

Peaches are so refreshing that, even though I'm allergic, I still eat it all the time.

Strawberry

Strawberries are also a classic that are just so refreshingly beautiful. They are so aesthetic too.

Kiwi

Kiwis are also SO much fun to eat. Every time I eat a kiwi, I feel rewarded after scooping it out of the skin.

Blueberry

Blueberries are the perfect on the go fruit. They're so small and don't make a mess when u bite into them.

Cherry

Cherries are so much fun to eat. It tastes delicious and you get a cute red/pink lip in the process. Although, you do have to spit out the pit.

Orange

ORANGES. just the best.
